France

France is a team that has a strong record in international soccer, having won the World Cup once before in 1998. Led by captain and star player Hugo Lloris, the French team will be a force to be reckoned with in the 2018 World Cup.

Brazil

Brazil is one of the most successful soccer nations in the world, having won the World Cup a record five times. With a strong attacking style and a roster filled with talented players like Neymar and Philippe Coutinho, Brazil will be a top contender in the 2018 World Cup.

Germany

Germany is the reigning World Cup champion, having won the 2014 tournament in Brazil. With a strong track record in international competitions and a talented roster featuring players like Mesut Ozil and Toni Kroos, Germany will be a team to watch in the 2018 World Cup.

Spain

Spain is a soccer powerhouse, having won the 2010 World Cup and finishing as runners-up in the 2018 tournament. With a possession-based style and a roster featuring players like Sergio Ramos and Andres Iniesta, Spain will be a team to watch in the 2018 World Cup.

Argentina

Argentina is a soccer-mad nation with a rich history in the sport. Led by star player Lionel Messi, Argentina will be looking to make a deep run in the 2018 World Cup and add to its impressive record of two World Cup titles.

Portugal

Portugal is a team that has a strong record in international competition, having won the European Championship in 2016. Led by star player Cristiano Ronaldo, Portugal will be looking to make a splash in the 2018 World Cup and add to its impressive trophy case.
